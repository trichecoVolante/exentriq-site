Automated Replenishment

Automated and assisted generation of replenishment orders within a collaborative environment — maximizing margins, minimizing stockouts, and giving buyers full oversight through advanced dashboards and mobile tools.
How It Works
Process Flow
AI-optimized orders from analysis to store delivery — maximizing margins.
1
Stock Analysis
Current inventory and sales patterns are analyzed to identify replenishment needs.
2
AI Optimization
Machine learning ensures optimal product assortment, maximizing margins per store.
3
Order Generation
Replenishment orders are created automatically and synced with the company's ERP.
4
Store Delivery
Mobile app enables store teams to track and manage incoming orders in real time.
5
Buyer Dashboard
Advanced dashboards give Buyers full oversight and decision support.
Solution Overview
Key results & capabilities
Automated and assisted generation of replenishment orders within a collaborative environment.
AI-powered order generation tools ensure optimal product assortment for each store, maximizing profit margins.
Native integration with the company’s ERP system enables automatic order creation and synchronization of key information flows.
Mobile application available in every store for tracking and managing replenishment orders in real time.
Advanced dashboard for Buyers, providing comprehensive oversight and decision support throughout the entire reordering process.
